API Endpoints

Generate Workout Plan

POST /api/fitness/generateWorkoutPlan

Creates a personalized workout plan based on your goals, fitness level, and preferences.

Exercise Details

POST /api/fitness/exerciseDetails

Provides detailed information about specific exercises, including proper form and target muscles.

Nutrition Advice

POST /api/fitness/nutritionAdvice

Delivers personalized nutrition guidance based on your goals and dietary preferences.

Custom Meal Plan

POST /api/fitness/customMealPlan

Generates detailed meal plans with recipes and nutritional information.

Analyze Food Plate

POST /api/fitness/analyzeFoodPlate

Analyzes the nutritional content of your meals based on the food items.

Carnivore Tips

POST /api/fitness/carnivoreTips

Provides specialized tips and guidance for following a carnivore-focused approach.
